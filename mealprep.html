Plan Smarter, Eat Better

Weekly Planning

Create a meal plan for the week to save time and avoid unhealthy choices.

  • Choose simple recipes
  • Make a grocery list
  • Prep ingredients early

Batch Cooking

Cook multiple meals at once to save time during the week.

  • Cook proteins in bulk
  • Use large pans or ovens
  • Store in portions

Storage Tips

  • Use airtight containers
  • Label meals with dates
  • Keep fridge organized

Balanced Meals

  • Protein (chicken, fish, beans)
  • Carbs (rice, quinoa)
  • Healthy fats (avocado, nuts)
